About Us

FUNacho is an international brand of cheese, chili, chips and condiments that provides consistent premium products and customer recognition. We began as a network of independent territory franchisees in 1988. Over the past 30 years, we have evolved into a multi-million-dollar company utilizing global distributors to service our growing list of international customers.

CHEESE

We developed portion packets as a direct result of customer feedback. Our clients wanted a microwaveable option to standard portion cheese cups. In addition, they demanded alternative sizing.

chili

The industry standard chili contains no real meat. Our customers challenged us to produce a premium chili with real beef. The result is delicious. Our chili packets contain 30% real beef – the highest percentage of real beef allowed for a shelf stable chili condiment.
